# METHODOLOGY

## Publication Evaluation Standards

Each publication was evaluated according to the following criteria:

### Tier Classification

**Tier 1 - Gold Standard (Major National/International Media)**
- National television network affiliates (ESPN, Fox, NBC, CBS, ABC)
- Major sports magazines with national distribution (Sports Illustrated)
- Wire services with global reach (Associated Press, Reuters)
- Major newspaper sports sections (New York Times, USA Today)
- **Threshold:** 50+ million monthly visitors OR national broadcast reach

**Tier 2 - Strong/Industry (Professional Publications)**
- Official league and team websites (NFL.com, team sites)
- Premium sports analysis platforms (247Sports, Pro Football Reference)
- Industry-leading statistical databases
- Regional major newspapers
- **Threshold:** 5-50 million monthly visitors OR industry authority status

**Tier 3 - Supplementary (Regional/Specialty)**
- Regional sports coverage
- Team-specific fan sites with editorial standards
- Specialty sports blogs with established credibility
- Local newspaper sports sections
- **Threshold:** Established editorial standards, verifiable authorship

### Evaluation Metrics

For each publication, the following were assessed:
- Publication name and type
- Geographic reach (local, regional, national, international)
- Estimated audience/circulation
- Editorial standards
- Article focus and depth
- Author attribution
- Significance to O-1A criterion
