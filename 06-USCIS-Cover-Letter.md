## Introduction

Innovative Global Accelerator Studios LLC ("Petitioner") respectfully submits this petition on Form I-129 seeking O-1A nonimmigrant classification on behalf of **Alex Hale** ("Beneficiary"), an Australian national who has demonstrated extraordinary ability in athletics as a professional American football placekicker.

The Beneficiary has achieved sustained national acclaim in his field through elite statistical performance at the collegiate level, multiple prestigious award recognitions, and extensive coverage in major national media outlets. He currently holds P-1A status and seeks to transition to O-1A classification to engage in athletic activities on behalf of the Petitioner.

This petition is supported by substantial documentary evidence demonstrating that Mr. Hale satisfies at least five (5) of the eight (8) regulatory criteria set forth at 8 CFR § 214.2(o)(3)(iii), well exceeding the minimum requirement of three (3) criteria.

---

## Beneficiary Qualifications Summary

Alex Hale is a 26-year-old professional athlete who has risen to national prominence in American football placekicking through extraordinary athletic ability and dedication. His qualifications include:

**Elite Athletic Performance:**
- Led the FBS (NCAA Division I Football Bowl Subdivision) in made field goals per game in 2023 (2.22)
- Career 79.6% field goal accuracy at Oklahoma State University, ranking 5th in program history
- 27 field goals in 2023, the second-highest single-season total in Oklahoma State history
- Starting placekicker for Oklahoma State University (Big 12 Conference) during 2020 and 2023 seasons
- Signed by the Green Bay Packers (NFL) and designated as International Pathway Player in 2024

**National Award Recognition:**
- **Two-time Lou Groza Award Semifinalist** (2020, 2023) – The Lou Groza Award recognizes the nation's premier collegiate placekicker; semifinalist status places the recipient among the top 20 kickers nationally
- **William V. Campbell Trophy Semifinalist** (2023) – Known as the "Academic Heisman," recognizing the nation's top scholar-athlete
- **CSC Academic All-America First Team** (2023) – The only placekicker named to the first team among 27 FBS selections
- **Four-time Academic All-Big 12 First Team** (2020-2023)

**Unique Background:**
Mr. Hale's journey to American football is extraordinary. Born in Australia, he was a Junior World Champion in wakeskating before relocating to the United States with no prior American football experience. He trained under legendary NFL kicker John Carney and walked on at Oklahoma State University, earning a scholarship through merit. Despite suffering a career-threatening ACL tear in his plant leg in 2020, he returned to achieve FBS statistical leadership in 2023—a remarkable demonstration of extraordinary ability and resilience.
