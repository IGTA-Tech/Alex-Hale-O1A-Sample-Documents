## Potential RFE Questions and Prepared Responses

### Q1: "How does the International Pathway Program constitute 'membership' under the regulation?"

**Prepared Response:**
The NFL International Player Pathway program is a selective association of elite international athletes, comparable to traditional membership associations because: (1) it requires outstanding achievement as judged by NFL experts (scouts, coaches, executives); (2) only 8-10 players are selected annually across the entire league; (3) selection involves evaluation against competitive peers. Under 8 CFR § 214.2(o)(3)(v), this constitutes comparable evidence where traditional membership associations do not exist in the beneficiary's field.

### Q2: "How do athletic achievements constitute 'original contributions of major significance'?"

**Prepared Response:**
Under the comparable evidence provision at 8 CFR § 214.2(o)(3)(v), athletic contributions of major significance are analogous to scholarly or business contributions. The Beneficiary's FBS-leading statistical performance, program records, and direct contribution to competitive success demonstrate significance comparable to traditional contributions in other fields. Expert letters from coaches and mentors attest to the major significance of these achievements within the field.
